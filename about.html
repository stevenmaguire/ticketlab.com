Built by sports fans for sports fans

Ticketlab is not only a search engine for sports tickets, but we also notify you via e-mail or SMS text when tickets become available that match your search criteria.

Our team

Iman Jalali

Founder

iman@ticketlab.com Linkedin Twitter

Iman originally started Ticketlab after looking online for a site that would allow you to search all remaining games for the Chicago Bulls and show you the best deals on courtside seats. He soon found out that no site existed that allows sports fans to do this, so he decided to start it himself. For about seven years prior Iman helped grow TrainSignal to approximately sixty employees as president until selling the company in August of 2013.

Steven Maguire

Co-Founder

steven@ticketlab.com Linkedin Twitter

Steven originally offered Iman a shoulder to cry on after learning about the travesty that was the lack of effective search for sports tickets. After drying his shirt and sharing in the success of TrainSignal, he began to explore the technical undertaking involved in creating a new platform for discovering great deals to sporting events. He partnered with Iman and delivers incremental improvements to the Ticketlab platform every day.
